# Nepal Digital Tourism Infrastructure
## Complete Documentation Package

---

## About This Package

This comprehensive documentation package presents a unified vision for establishing **Nepal's National Digital Tourism Infrastructure** — a subscription-based platform enabling all 753 local governments (Palikas) to manage and present their tourism and civic assets professionally.

**Created**: December 2025  
**Purpose**: Government proposals, RFPs, stakeholder presentations, policy discussions  
**Format**: Interconnected markdown documents

## Key Messages (Across All Documents)

### The Core Problem
> Nepal's rich tourism assets remain digitally invisible, while Palikas struggle with fragmented systems, unpredictable costs, and procurement risk.

### The Solution
> A standardized, subscription-based digital infrastructure platform that empowers all 753 Palikas with affordable, professional digital presence — while ensuring government ownership and policy alignment.

### The Innovation
> We're not selling software. We're offering infrastructure-as-a-service — like electricity or telecommunications — with transparent, audit-safe subscription pricing.

### The Value
> Local empowerment + National coordination + Cultural preservation + Economic opportunity + Risk reduction + Long-term sustainability

### The Differentiation
> Two service bundles (Tourism-focused & Civic Services) on one platform, enabling every Palika to start where they are and grow when ready.
